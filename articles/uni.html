Those who entered a prestige university by means of their birth, parents or money will never understand the feelings of those who strive to enter with minimal resources available. Some talented students cannot enter the university due to the reasons unrelated to their studying abilities. Destitute stifles growth.

You have to sleep to memorize the material you study.

You have to sleep to restore your body.

If you don’t have enough sleep, you are inviting a catastrophe;

Chernobyl and disease come from the lack of sleep.

You should drop or postpone as many projects as you can.

Hire men. Men come out cheaper.

Pre-requisites for learning underscore the problem of initial inequality and conditions for studying:

  • • healthy food
  • • a quiet place to study and to rest. Nobody can accustom oneself to noise, noise keeps irritating more and more. It is almost impossible to excel in many fields without prolonged seclusion. It takes place in concentrated isolation too, as do other things that drive success, such as strategic planning and rest. Talent will necessitate a social retreat. This is quest for social status through other means.
  • • time for studying and sleep. Transportation and long travelling distances can take a huge amount of time and strength that could be used for studying.
  • • support. Studying is a lengthy process with many years ahead.
  • • excellent teachers and study materials in order not to waste 5 times more resources on re-learning.
  • • organized and supervised friendly environment with high moral standards
  • • a Virtuous State which raises benevolent men and women
